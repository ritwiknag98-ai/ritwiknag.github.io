Research Focus & Technical Expertise

  • Crystal Growth: Growth of detector-grade wide-bandgap single-crystal semiconductors using multiple techniques, including Bridgman and vertical gradient freeze methods.
  • Device Fabrication: Fabrication of semiconductor devices using DC sputtering and thermal evaporation, along with surface passivation oxide growth using pulsed laser deposition (PLD) and chemical vapor deposition (CVD).
  • Device & Material Characterization: Electrical and structural characterization including I–V, C–V, SEM, EDX, XPS, XRD, Raman spectroscopy, UV–Vis spectroscopy, Hall effect/van der Pauw measurements, AFM, and advanced defect characterization using DLTS, CTS, and PICTS techniques.
  • Modeling & Data Analysis: Experience with data analysis and device modeling tools including Origin, LTSpice, MATLAB, 1D Poisson Solver, Ansys HFSS, Synopsys Sentaurus, VESTA, R-Studio, and LabVIEW.
  • Radiation Detection: Radiation detection using fabricated semiconductor devices for evaluation of real-world, room-temperature radiation detector performance.
